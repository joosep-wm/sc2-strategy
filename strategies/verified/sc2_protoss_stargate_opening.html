Protoss Stargate Opening: Double-Gate Adept into Oracle

Two-gate Adept pressure, expand behind it, Oracle follow-up. The harass pays twice: economic damage and a full scout. The opening ends at 5:30; the endgame is decided by what the Adepts and Oracle saw, not by this page.

Harass pays twice

Two Adepts and an Oracle force damage AND read the enemy opening: gates, gas count, tech, expand or not. Even a zero-kill shade is a full scout.

Probes are the build

8 → 40 workers by 6:00 in the source game, while harassing. Every chrono goes to probes until ~5:30, then to upgrade structures. Never to unit production.

Chrono rule

Every cast on probes until ~5:30, then upgrade buildings only. Never unit production. (Source game: five Nexus casts, then Cyber, Forge, Fleet Beacon, all game.)

Micro notes

  • Adepts: shade in from outside vision; cancel if Stalkers camp the landing. Zero kills is still a full scout.
  • Oracle: enter from the map edge, not down the ramp. Kill workers until units respond, scout tech on the way out. Keep it alive: it is Revelation and detection later.
  • Probes: the only cuts sit around the Stargate and Nexus payments. Banking 400+ minerals means the build broke.

Resource gates (why the timings look like this)

The 400-mineral Nexus at 3:12 empties the bank (20 minerals). The Oracle is affordable from ~3:35; gas is never the constraint (200+ banked by then).

Decision point at 5:30 — what the harass told you

Scouted: expand, 1-2 gates, robo or twilight standard macro game

Your pick. The source game's follow-up: +2 Stargates + Fleet Beacon at 5:40 into mass Void Ray (skip Warp Gate). Or Twilight into Blink (research Warp Gate now).

Second Shield Battery, Stalkers instead of a second Oracle, Adepts home, research Warp Gate for reinforcement. Hold: you out-eco after.

Scouted: Stargate mirror oracle / void rays coming

Stalkers + Battery at home, Oracle stays defensive, consider a Phoenix switch.

Warp Gate rule: research it (50/50, 100f at the Cyber) the moment a branch makes Gateways your production. Skip it while Stargates are producing; going mass air it may never be worth the cost.
