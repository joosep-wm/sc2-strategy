2v2 Proxy Rax + Gateway → 2-Base Timing

Terran + Protoss synced opening. The proxy is not the win condition. It is scouting, greed punishment, and surprise damage. The real punch is the coordinated 2-base timing.

Terran: 1 Proxy Barracks Protoss: 1 Proxy Gateway Goal: 2-base timing wins Do not accidentally become an all-in clown car
Warning — timing-verified, not results-proven

The proxy opening did not work well in practice

Every timing on this page is DB-reproducible against patch 5.0.16, which is why it stays in the verified set. But in actual games the proxy Rax/Gate opening underperformed: the pressure rarely paid for its cost and the disruption it bought was thin. Treat this build as experimental — the numbers are sound, the results are not. If you want a proven line, prefer the straight 2-base timing without the proxy.

Proxy purpose

Scout both openings, punish fast naturals, force panic spending, and create uncertainty.

Attack force

Real first hit: ~5 Marines + 3 Zealots, ready ~3:15–3:30 from one Barracks + one Gateway. The 1–2 units at ~2:00–2:15 are poke/scout, not the punch.

Discipline rule

Stop reinforcing unless a kill is clearly available. Expand, gas, tech, then hit together.

Read the enemy opening

  • Double fast expand: continue pressure plan.
  • Gas/unit expand: poke, force defense, do not donate units.
  • One-base/no natural: prepare defense immediately.

Gather, do not trickle

Do not attack with 1 Marine + 1 Zealot unless you only need scouting.

By now (~2:30) you only have ~2–3 Marines + 1 Zealot. Keep massing — the dangerous ball is the 5th–6th Marine at ~3:15.

~5:30–6:30

2-base timing push

This is the actual win condition.

  • Move together, not two heroic solo tragedies.
  • Terran bio provides DPS and anti-air.
  • Protoss gateway/tech units tank, zone, and break positions.
  • Hit before enemy splash/air/late tech becomes comfortable.
~5:30 lean (Stim + more Marines + 1 Medivac, double gas from ~3:00) or ~6:00–6:30 full (Stim + 2 Medivac + extra production). One geyser can't fund the full package by 5:30.

Proxy has done its job

If the proxy still exists, nice. If it died after scouting and forcing defense, also nice.

The win condition is the synced 2-base army, not keeping a Barracks and Gateway alive like beloved family pets.

When the opener is successful

  • Expansion cancelled or delayed.
  • Workers killed or pulled for a long time.
  • Bunker, Battery, Spine, or extra units forced.
  • Enemy strategy identified early.
  • Your 2-base timing lands against a disrupted team.

When to abort the pressure

  • Both enemies are one-basing.
  • Static defense is complete and efficient.
  • Speedlings, Queens, Bunkers, or Batteries make the fight bad.
  • Your naturals are threatened.
  • You are tempted to keep making proxy units “just because”.

Best enemy openings to punish

  • Double fast expand.
  • One greedy player + one light defender.
  • Slow tech without early units.
  • Bad wall-offs or exposed naturals.

Risky enemy openings

  • Double one-base aggression.
  • Fast Zergling pressure.
  • Proxy/cannon rush if not scouted early.
  • Hard turtle where pressure gets zero damage.
