Chrono rule (unchanged doctrine)

Every cast on probes until the Bay finishes (~6:00), then Colossus + Thermal Lance only. Never Gateways.

Defense checkpoints

  • 1:30 probe read: missing workers / proxy structures / 6+ lings on the map = rush. Call it in chat immediately.
  • Rush called: 2nd Battery, Immortals instead of Bay, MadSysop bunkers the wall. Hold, then counter with the same 2-base army at ~9:30.
  • 5:20 Observer read: their worker count and base count decide the target: hit the greedier opponent.
  • Nobody fights alone before 8:30. Poke range only. The Undercurrent loss (dead at 4:30, tech instead of Battery) is the anti-pattern.
