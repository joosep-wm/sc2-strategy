Dante's standing rule

Standard macro open every game: Nexus, Gateway, Assimilator, Cyber Core, Warp Gate. Build 6 Stalkers for early defense while you take the second base and tech. First robo unit is always an Observer, then an Immortal.

Why double robo

  • Observer gives permanent detection before the DTs, so you never get opened up.
  • Immortal is a defender you point and click, no micro tax.
  • Two robos + Bay means Colossi arrive fast enough to matter and keep coming.

Resource note

These are producer-start landmarks, not resource-gated frames. If a structure is not affordable at its slot, finish the current probe pump first. Banking 500+ minerals means you cut too hard.

The one thing that loses the game

Danger, the DT window is 5:00

Your Observer at 4:34 is your life insurance

Do not skip the Observer for a faster Colossus. The first Colossus lands at 6:40, which is far too late to defend the Dark Templar hit. Detection up before 5:00 is non-negotiable, the Immortal and Stalkers do the killing once the DTs are lit up.

Against the DT rush

Two things beat a Dark Templar rush: detection by ~4:30 and something that shoots by 5:00. You have both here, the Observer at 4:34 and six Stalkers already home, with the Immortal at 5:13 as backup. In 2v2, a partner Terran Scan is free instant detection, so lean on it if your Observer is late or dies. Do not chase the DT player's workers, his economy is already spent on the tech, just hold and out-macro him.
