Stargate Oracle Expand: scout, harass, expand

A forgiving eco-tempo line. The Oracle finds the enemy plan, chips their mineral line with Pulsar Beam, and defends your own while you drop a second Nexus behind it. Slightly more active than a pure macro build, still low on the micro tax.

Protoss 2v2 low-micro patch 5.0.16

One unit, three jobs

The Oracle scouts the enemy tech, harasses workers with Pulsar Beam, and defends your mineral lines. It earns its cost even on a zero-kill flyover.

Expand behind it

The Oracle buys map information and tempo, so the second Nexus goes down safely behind the harass instead of blind.

Read, then commit

Follow into Gateway units and a robo if you scout cloak, or keep the Stargate rolling into Void Rays if the game stays macro.

The core plan

Nexus, Gateway, Assimilator, Cyber Core, Stargate, Oracle, then expand. Use the Oracle to scout the enemy for DT tech or an early all-in, and defend your mineral lines with Pulsar Beam.

Oracle abilities

  • Pulsar Beam is your attack, brilliant against workers and light units, and it can shoot down at a harasser hitting your base.
  • Revelation reveals an area including cloaked units, but it is soft, temporary detection and does not let your army target them once it fades.

Resource note

These are producer-start landmarks, not resource-gated frames. If the Oracle or Nexus is not affordable at its slot, finish the current probe pump first, then place it. Do not fly the Oracle into anti-air blind.

The one thing that loses the game

Danger, revelation is not a defense

Oracle Revelation is temporary detection

Revelation reveals an area for a few seconds, it is not a permanent detector against a committed Dark Templar rush. If you scout a Dark Shrine, get real detection, an Observer or a Photon Cannon, up by 5:00. The Oracle is your early warning, not your answer.

Against the DT rush

The Oracle's Revelation gives you temporary detection, useful for catching the tech early, but it is not a substitute for a permanent detector against a committed DT rush. If you scout the tech, add a robo for an Observer or a Photon Cannon at each base and have it up by 5:00. In 2v2, a partner Terran Scan is free instant detection, so coordinate the scan on your base the moment the Oracle spots a Dark Shrine. Treat the Oracle as the early-warning scout and back it with real detection.
