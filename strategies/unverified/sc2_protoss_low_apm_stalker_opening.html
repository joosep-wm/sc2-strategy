Low-APM Opening: Continuous Stalker + Natural Battery

One gate never idles, one Battery covers the natural, zero shade micro. Replaces the double-Adept opening for players whose APM budget goes to probes and pylons. First defender out at 2:45, before the earliest harass observed in 60 replays (2:49).

Why this build exists

Harass was deciding games

A kill in our base before 5:00 meant a 25% winrate vs 62% without, with 12 workers lost on average before 7:00. The old opening sent both Adepts away at 2:55, right as harass arrived.

Units came too late

First gateway unit at median 3:12, only 2 combat units by 4:00. This build has a Stalker at 2:45 and six by 5:00, from one gateway, with no micro.

One geyser funds it

Stalker cadence from one gate needs ~111 gas/min; one geyser with 3 probes yields ~114 (DB mining model). Gas #2 is entirely for the tech path.

Rules

Chrono on probes only until 5:30.

Unchanged from the verified Stargate opening.

The gate is never idle.

If gas dips, insert a Zealot (100m, 0g, 27f) instead of waiting for 50 gas.

Low-APM Warp Gate.

After Warp Gate research completes, an untransformed Gateway produces about 40% faster (DB note). Most of the benefit, zero warp-in micro. Transform (25/25 each in 5.0.16) only when you want forward warp-ins.

The 3:30 scout rule.

Enemy naturals start at median 3:06 (66 opponents measured). If the probe sees NO enemy natural by 3:30: second Battery immediately, Stalkers from both gates, no tech until 6:00. Observed one-base all-ins ended games between 4:57 and 6:02.

No second tech building before 5:30. Ever.

Tech sprawl was the number one loss pattern in the 2v2 replay set.

The Zerg gas read (added after the 2026-07-12 Rainfall loss).

Zerg on 4 or more Extractors by ~4:30 with few units on the map is committing gas to tech, usually Mutalisks (22 Mutas cost 2200 gas; the Rainfall Zerg ran 7 Extractors). Structural timing from the DB: a Spire started at time T can produce Mutas from about T+1:30 (Spire 66f + Muta 24f) plus travel; observed: Spire 5:33, hit 8:55. Response, all inside the templar path: 1 Photon Cannon per mineral line (150m, 29f, the Forge is already there for upgrades), the 6 opening Stalkers stay home as the screen, and dump every banked 150 gas into a High Templar the moment gas passes 300. Merge Archons with any HT that lacks Storm energy. When the flock arrives: probes to the Battery, army holds the mineral line, never chase Mutas outward, the ground follow-up is coming.
